A COLD YELLOW BEVERAGE

Refreshing on a hot summer day when you are ready to eat or enjoy some heat.

Ingrediants

  • ice
  • cherry
  • tequila
  • sour mix

Recipe instructions

  1. put ice into a cup
  2. in another cup mix your tequila and sour mix at a 2:1 ratio
  3. shake and shake
  4. pour liquid into cup with ice and enjoy with a cherry
